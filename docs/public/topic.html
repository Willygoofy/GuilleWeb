Star Wars

In this page I am going to talk about the Star Wars saga

We are going to divide this topic in two main parts: the films and the tv series

Films

When we talk about Star Wars film we you must take into account two diferent types of Star Wars films

Firts we are going to talk about the main saga

EPISODE I

The Phantom Menace

The Galactic Republic is in chaos. Taxes on trade routes to outer star systems are in dispute. Hoping to settle the matter with a blockade of powerful warships, the greedy Trade Federation has stopped all shipments to the small planet of Naboo.

EPISODE II

Attack of the Clones

Unrest reigns in the Galactic Senate. Several thousand solar systems have declared their intention to leave the Republic. This separatist movement, led by the mysterious Count Dooku, has made it difficult for the limited number of Jedi Knights to maintain peace and order in the galaxy. Senator Amidala, former Queen of Naboo, returns to the Galactic Senate to cast her vote on the critical issue of creating a Republic army to aid the overwhelmed Jedi.

EPISODE III

Revenge of the Sith

War! The Republic crumbles under the attacks of the ruthless Sith Lord, Count Dooku. There are heroes on both sides, but evil is everywhere. In a decisive move, the diabolical droid leader, General Grievous, has stormed the capital of the Republic and kidnapped Chancellor Palpatine, leader of the Galactic Senate. As the Separatist droid army tries to flee with their valuable hostage, two Jedi Knights begin a desperate mission to free the captive Chancellor.

EPISODE VI

A New Hope

The ship in which Princess Leia is traveling is captured by imperial troops under the command of the fearsome Darth Vader. Before being caught, Leia manages to enter a message into her robot R2-D2, who, accompanied by her inseparable C-3PO, manages to escape. After landing on the planet Tattooine, they are captured and sold to young Luke Skywalker, who will discover the hidden message that is intended for Obi Wan Kenobi, the Jedi master whom Luke must find to save the princess.

EPISODE V

The Empire Strikes Back

These are adverse times for rebellion. Although the Death Star has been destroyed, Imperial troops have driven the rebel forces out of their hidden bases and pursue them across the galaxy. After escaping the terrible Imperial Fleet, a group of freedom warriors, led by Luke Skywalker, have established a new secret base on the remote frozen world of Hoth.

EPISODE VI

Return of the Jedi

Six months after Han Solo was captured and frozen in carbonite in Cloud City, the Galactic Empire is building a new Death Star. Meanwhile, young Luke fights to achieve Jedi mastery.

EPISODE VII

The Force Awakens

Thirty years after the Rebel Alliance's victory over the second Death Star, the galaxy has to face a new threat: the evil Kylo Ren and the First Order. When the deserter Finn arrives on a desert planet he meets Rey, whose android contains a secret map. Together, the young couple will join forces with Han Solo to ensure that the resistance finds Luke Skywalker, the last of the Jedi Knights.

EPISODE VIII

The Last Jedi

The First Order, under the command of Supreme Leader Snoke, is about to seize military control of the galaxy. Only General Leia Organa and her Resistance fighters stand up to the growing tyranny, convinced that Jedi Master Luke Skywalker will return and restore a spark of hope to the fight.

EPISODE IX

The Rise of Skywalker

The surviving Resistance faces the First Order, and Rey, Finn, Poe and the rest of the heroes will face new challenges and a final battle with the wisdom of previous generations.
