Star Wars

In this page I am going to talk about the Star Wars saga

We are going to divide this topic in two main parts: the films and the tv series

Films

When we talk about Star Wars film we you must take into account two diferent types of Star Wars films

Firts we are going to talk about the main saga

EPISODE I

The Phantom Menace

The Galactic Republic is in chaos. Taxes on trade routes to outer star systems are in dispute. Hoping to settle the matter with a blockade of powerful warships, the greedy Trade Federation has stopped all shipments to the small planet of Naboo.

EPISODE II

Attack of the Clones

Unrest reigns in the Galactic Senate. Several thousand solar systems have declared their intention to leave the Republic. This separatist movement, led by the mysterious Count Dooku, has made it difficult for the limited number of Jedi Knights to maintain peace and order in the galaxy. Senator Amidala, former Queen of Naboo, returns to the Galactic Senate to cast her vote on the critical issue of creating a Republic army to aid the overwhelmed Jedi.

EPISODE III

Revenge of the Sith

War! The Republic crumbles under the attacks of the ruthless Sith Lord, Count Dooku. There are heroes on both sides, but evil is everywhere. In a decisive move, the diabolical droid leader, General Grievous, has stormed the capital of the Republic and kidnapped Chancellor Palpatine, leader of the Galactic Senate. As the Separatist droid army tries to flee with their valuable hostage, two Jedi Knights begin a desperate mission to free the captive Chancellor.

EPISODE VI

A New Hope

The ship in which Princess Leia is traveling is captured by imperial troops under the command of the fearsome Darth Vader. Before being caught, Leia manages to enter a message into her robot R2-D2, who, accompanied by her inseparable C-3PO, manages to escape. After landing on the planet Tattooine, they are captured and sold to young Luke Skywalker, who will discover the hidden message that is intended for Obi Wan Kenobi, the Jedi master whom Luke must find to save the princess.

EPISODE V

The Empire Strikes Back

These are adverse times for rebellion. Although the Death Star has been destroyed, Imperial troops have driven the rebel forces out of their hidden bases and pursue them across the galaxy. After escaping the terrible Imperial Fleet, a group of freedom warriors, led by Luke Skywalker, have established a new secret base on the remote frozen world of Hoth.

EPISODE VI

Return of the Jedi

Six months after Han Solo was captured and frozen in carbonite in Cloud City, the Galactic Empire is building a new Death Star. Meanwhile, young Luke fights to achieve Jedi mastery.

EPISODE VII

The Force Awakens

Thirty years after the Rebel Alliance's victory over the second Death Star, the galaxy has to face a new threat: the evil Kylo Ren and the First Order. When the deserter Finn arrives on a desert planet he meets Rey, whose android contains a secret map. Together, the young couple will join forces with Han Solo to ensure that the resistance finds Luke Skywalker, the last of the Jedi Knights.

EPISODE VIII

The Last Jedi

The First Order, under the command of Supreme Leader Snoke, is about to seize military control of the galaxy. Only General Leia Organa and her Resistance fighters stand up to the growing tyranny, convinced that Jedi Master Luke Skywalker will return and restore a spark of hope to the fight.

EPISODE IX

The Rise of Skywalker

The surviving Resistance faces the First Order, and Rey, Finn, Poe and the rest of the heroes will face new challenges and a final battle with the wisdom of previous generations.

We also have films that expand the story about the Star Wars cinematic universe

We have films such us

Rogue One:

A Star Wars Story

In a time of conflict, a group of atypical heroes join together on a suicide mission to steal the plans to "The Death Star", the Empire's ultimate weapon of destruction. This key event in the "Star Wars" timeline brings together ordinary people who decide to do extraordinary things and thus become part of something much more important than themselves.

Solo:

A Star Wars Story

Young Han Solo flees the planet Corellia years before meeting Luke Skywalker and Princess Leia, but he has to leave his beloved Qi'ra behind. After enrolling at the Imperial Academy to be a pilot, Han meets a Wookiee named Chewbacca and later the famous gamer Lando Calrissian, owner of the Millennium Falcon. On a desperate mission, Han will team up with Tobias Beckett to obtain a valuable shipment of coaxium.

TV Series

Besides the films, we also have tv series thar also expand the cinematic universe of Star Wars

We can divide the series in two main types: Live action and Animation

Animation

Star Wars

The Clone Wars

When the Clone War spreads across the galaxy, Anakin Skywalker and his new Padawan apprentice Ahsoka Tano accept a dangerous mission: rescue the son of crime lord Jabba the Hutt.

Star Wars

Rebels

Fourteen years after the fall of the Galactic Republic and the Jedi Order and the rise of the Galactic Empire, a ragtag group of rebels band together aboard a light freighter called the Ghost and begin conducting covert operations against Imperial forces in and around the planet Lothal and other planets threatened by the Empire.

Tales of the

Jedi

Each episode of Tales of the Jedi tells a short story featuring Jedi from the Star Wars prequel trilogy era. The six episodes featured in the series' first season are split into two "paths": the first following Ahsoka Tano across various points in her life, and the other depicting a young Count Dooku before his fall to the dark side of the Force.

Live action

Star Wars

The Mandalorian

Five years after the events of Return of the Jedi and the fall of the galactic empire, The Mandalorian follows Din Djarin, a lone Mandalorian bounty hunter in the far reaches of the galaxy. He is hired by the remaining Imperial forces to recover the child Grogu, but instead she runs away to protect him. As they seek to reunite Grogu with his kind, they are pursued by Moff Gideon, who wants to use Grogu's connection to the Force. For season three, the duo will travel to Mandalore so Din Djarin can redeem himself for the transgression of removing his helmet.

Obi-Wan

Kenobi

Ten years after the events of Star Wars: Episode III Revenge of the Sith (2005) in which the Jedi were purged with Order 66 and Obi-Wan Kenobi's apprentice, Anakin Skywalker, became the Sith Lord Darth Vader—Kenobi is in hiding on the planet Tatooine, watching over Anakin's son, Luke, when he is called on a mission to rescue Anakin's daughter, Leia, after she is kidnapped by the Galactic Empire's Jedi-hunting Inquisitors in a plot to draw Kenobi out. This leads to a confrontation between Kenobi and Vader.

Ashoka

Ahsoka Tano has had one of the biggest character arcs in all of Star Wars, both in-universe and out of it. First introduced in The Clone Wars movie, the previously unnamed Padawan of Anakin has become not only one of the most complex and essential characters in the franchise but one of the most beloved, to the point where she not only survived Order 66, but became an important part of Rebels and even had a voice appearance in The Rise of Skywalker prior to a live-action debut in season two of The Mandalorian. Now she’s the first animated Star Wars character to lead their own live-action show, with Rosario Dawson stepping into the title role, and Dave Filoni, who co-created the character with George Lucas, at the helm to navigate 15 years’ worth of backstory and character development.
